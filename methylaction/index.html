MethylAction

Detecting differentially methylated regions (DMRs) that distinguish biological and disease subtypes

What is MethylAction?

MethylAction is an R package for detecting differentially methylated regions (DMRs) from enrichment-based techniques for sequencing DNA methlyation genome-wide. These techniques include MBD-isolated Genome Sequencing (MiGS), MBD-seq, and MeDIP-seq.

Key Features:

  • Designed for studies investigating biological and disease sub-types
  • Supports comparisons between more than 2 groups (up to 8 tested by authors)
  • Computes statistical significance across genomic regions
  • Stratifies groups by consistency ("frequent" and "other" DMRs)
  • Performs bootstrapping or permutations to establish false discovery rates (FDRs)
  • Provides tools for visualization (heatmaps and karyograms)
